This page is intended to provide space to focus on presenting a subject in more detail. The current focus of this page is to present my sconce lighting designs.
My sconces serve as covers for a separate light source. These covers attach to a wall at two different points, at a minimum, but can have four points total, depending upon what is needed by the customer. While they are intended to be easy to mount, that may present a problem in that someone could lift it off the wall and take the sconce. To address that, the means to secure them to the wall are provided.
