Sconce Information

These handcrafted sconces combine glass designs with copper, creating unique and eye-catching art for your home. This is a sconce-style cover to place over a light bulb on a wall. The face of this cover is made of 16 oz. hammered copper with a glass lens to allow light through. It mounts to the wall on two screws which align with keyhole mounting locations on the back side. Screws are included. The copper is coated with Incralac so the finish will be preserved both indoors or outdoors. The back side has a coated steel housing by which it is mounted to a wall. Watch the  Installation Video  to find out more. 

A compact profile light socket is needed as shown in the photo and video. The photo from 1000bulbs.com shows the socket used for my displays. For this particular glass design a 40w equivalent medium base LED bulb is suggested, however customer preference may require a different wattage which must be purchased by the customer. An alternative design, which addresses concerns about complying with dark sky requirements, prevents light from being directed straight upward and is available for the same price. Also, tamperproof screws (included) can be used to secure the sconce to wall to prevent theft. Determine if a permanent weather cap needs to be added if you will be placing the sconce in locations not protected from rain.

This product is only shipped to the lower forty eight states by UPS Ground. Depending upon availability at time of order, this product may need to be built first, so allow 4-6 weeks for delivery.
